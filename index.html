About B S Fire Service

Delivering reliable, certified, and high-quality fire protection solutions since 2023

Our Journey

Originally established as BS Construction in 2017, the company expanded its vision and expertise into the fire safety sector with the establishment of B S Fire Service in 2023. Owned and managed by Mr. Bakhya Bishal Kashyap (B.Tech), the company is built on strong technical knowledge, industry experience, and a commitment to safety excellence.

B S Fire Service is a trusted fire safety equipment supply and installation company dedicated to protecting lives and property. We provide reliable, certified, and high-quality fire protection solutions for:

  • Residential Buildings
  • Commercial Establishments
  • Industrial Units
  • Institutional Premises

Our Mission

"Safety First – Protection Always."

With trained technicians and industry-compliant systems, we ensure that every project meets required fire safety norms and regulatory standards. Our goal is to deliver dependable protection systems that provide safety, compliance, and long-term peace of mind.
